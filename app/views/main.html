Frequently Ask Questions

These are some basic questions/answers we thought our user may need while using our Mapstrack app

What is MapsTrack?

MapsTrack is a location finder app with the motto to reduce the difficulty in finding address when going to party, marriage or meeting a friend. Just share the event or location with your friend in your contact list who you want to invite, your location or event is automatically shared with that friend with map directing location of the place where event is taking place.

What is difference between events and location?

Locations are your unique address which a user create for inviting someone at their place, it may be shop, house, office while events are those location who have very specific purpose and expiry date.

How can I share my location to friends?

For sharing the location just add friends from your contact list and it will automatically shared to your friend, If your friend has mapstrack app then we will send a notification that "an event has been shared with you" while if your friend doesn't have app we will send a sms with access code with that he can reach you.

Is am I able to see my friends location?

Yes, if your friend has added you for seeing his location continuously then you can see his location in the share section of the Mapstrack app.

I am not able to search private event and location?

One cannot search private location and event as they are made with the motto to share within specific group of people, you can make any event or location private if you don't want anyone to search for it.

Where will I get the app from?

Mapstrack is an android app which can be easily downloaded from playstore, also you can get app link if you enter number and click on get app button of our site.
